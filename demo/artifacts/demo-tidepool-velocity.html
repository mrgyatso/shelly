What the differential shows

The premise: unitsSold is a lifetime counter, so the delta between two sightings is units sold in that window — a live sales rate to rank sellers by. Across 8,281 sellers seen twice about five hours apart on production data, unitsSold changed for none of them. Sellers listing thousands of items mid-session moved not a single unit.

A stale cache would explain that — except followerCount, read from the same seller record in the same catalog response, changed for 4,620 of those same sellers. The payload is demonstrably fresh. Only one of the two fields is frozen. unitsSold is not being updated on the cadence we sample at — if at all on the free tier.

Every velocity score in the product is a division by a delta that is always zero. The ranking isn't wrong at the margins. It has no signal in it at all.

What that breaks

The "Rising Sellers" board, the momentum email, and the API's sort=velocity parameter all resolve to the same order every run — whatever the tiebreaker happens to be. We've been shipping a stable but meaningless ranking and reading its stability as confidence. It never contradicted itself because it was never measuring anything to begin with.

What is genuinely healthy

The accumulator itself is fine. 12,397 sellers are indexed, the nightly refresh completed cleanly during this audit, and followerCount is live — it moves for over half of sellers within hours. Everything except the one derived metric is working. The fix is a metric decision, not a plumbing repair.

The fallback is already collected

Follower-delta momentum needs no new data. It's measurable today, from records already in the store, for 4,620 of 8,281 sellers in a five-hour window alone. If the product needs a "who's heating up" signal now, this is it — defensible, live, and free. Whether that's the right signal depends on what the ranking is actually for, which is the real question below.

Where the ranking goes from here

The metric is the product — this decides whether tidepool ships a momentum signal it can defend or keeps rendering a placebo. My recommendation: cut over to follower-delta now, because it's the only option that's proven-live and costs nothing, and treat the others as follow-ups once we know what the ranking is actually promising.
